💬 Dialogue

Vamos ao cinema?
Read through the dialogue. Then listen again and follow along.
A
Quando vocês vão ao cinema?
When are you (all) going to the movies?
B
Daqui a pouco, você quer ir?
Pretty soon — do you want to go?
A
Não sei, a Susana também vai?
I don't know — is Susana going too?
B
Vai sim.
Yes, she is.
A
Ah, então eu também vou.
Oh, then I'll go too.
🗣️ Ponto fonológico — Nasal "-ão": Notice how vão and então end in a nasal sound that doesn't exist in English. Say it slowly: vã-oo with your mouth nearly closed. It's the same vowel in não, irmão, pão.
🎬 Cinema no Brasil: Going to the movies (ir ao cinema) is very popular in Brazil's big cities. Cinemas usually show international films dubbed (dublado) or subtitled (legendado) — Brazilians often have a strong preference for one or the other! Snacks include pipoca (popcorn) and refrigerante (soda), just like everywhere else.
