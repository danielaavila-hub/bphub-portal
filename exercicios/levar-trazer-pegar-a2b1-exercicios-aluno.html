Produção
Write your own WhatsApp messages.
Write 5–7 messages for a group chat. You are organizing a dinner at your place. Ask people to bring things, tell someone to pick something up, and say what you'll take care of yourself. Use levar, trazer, and pegar at least once each.
Quem traz…? Você pode pegar… no mercado? Eu levo… Pego no caminho Traz também… Eu já peguei…

When you're done, send your text to your teacher. She will read it carefully and give you personalized feedback — pointing out what you did well and helping you improve your Portuguese even further. This is one of the best ways to grow as a writer in a new language!
