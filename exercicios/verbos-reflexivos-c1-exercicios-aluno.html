Context These exercises are based on the dialogue between Beatriz (personal trainer) and Rodrigo (executive) at a São Paulo gym. Review the material before completing these tasks. Exercises 1–4 are interactive. Exercise 5 is open-ended production.
1
Matching — Verbo e Contexto
What does each reflexive express in context?
Match each verb (left) with the best description of its contextual meaning (right). Type the letter in the box.
Verbo
1.me desligar
2.me virei
3.se dá bem
4.se arrepende
5.me descuidado
6.me separei de
7.me aproveitei de
8.me mudei
Significado
A — to move to a new place
B — to switch off mentally; stop thinking about stress
C — to get along well with someone
D — to regret something (always reflexive)
E — to take advantage of a situation opportunistically
F — to have been neglecting oneself
G — to part ways with a partner / split up
H — to manage on one's own; to figure things out
2
Escolha Contextual
Reflexive or not? Choose the correct form.
In each sentence, choose the form that fits the context. Pay attention to meaning — the difference is not always grammatical, but semantic.
3
Preencha — Novo Contexto
A different scene, same reflexive precision.
Fill in each blank with the correct reflexive verb form from the box. Conjugate as needed. Each verb is used once.
dedicar-se despedir-se divertir-se mudar-se recuperar-se esforçar-se concentrar-se animar-se
4
Correção de Erros
Each sentence has exactly one error. Rewrite it correctly.
These are errors typical of C1 learners — reflexive/non-reflexive confusion, body part rule violations, or European Portuguese patterns. Identify and correct the error in each sentence.
5
Produção
Write a dialogue using at least 8 reflexive verbs.
Write a 10–14 line dialogue between two people in a situation of your choice — a doctor's appointment, a job interview, a conversation between friends planning a trip. Include at least 8 reflexive verbs from this material. Underline or bold each reflexive verb you use. Aim for natural, idiomatic Brazilian Portuguese.
animar-se concentrar-se se virar arrepender-se de dedicar-se a dar-se bem mudar-se ★ despedir-se ★ divertir-se ★ descuidar-se
