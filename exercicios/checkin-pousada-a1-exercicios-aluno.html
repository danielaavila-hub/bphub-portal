Número + gênero (1 and 2)

Only 1 and 2 change with gender in Portuguese.
MasculinoFeminino
1um travesseirouma toalha
2dois travesseirosduas toalhas

Vocabulário do quarto

o = masculino  ·  a = feminino
o quarto (room) o travesseiro (pillow) o sabonete (soap) o elevador (elevator) a cama (bed) a toalha (towel) a coberta (blanket) a chave (key) a garrafa de água (bottle of water)

Part 2 — Controlled Practice

Exercise 1 — um / uma / dois / duas

Complete with the correct word. The quantity is in parentheses.
1. Eu gostaria de pedir (1) toalha, por favor.
I'd like a towel, please.
2. Eu gostaria de pedir (2) travesseiros.
I'd like two pillows.
3. Tem (1) elevador aqui?
Is there an elevator here?
4. Eu reservei (1) quarto para (2) noites.
I've booked a room for two nights.
5. Por favor, (2) garrafas de água.
Two bottles of water, please.
6. Eu gostaria de pedir (2) cobertas, por favor.
I'd like two blankets, please.

Part 3 — Written Production

A. Your check-in form

Complete your check-in form (use fake numbers). Then, practice saying the numbers in Portuguese out loud.
Formulário de Check-in

B. What do you need?

You just arrived at the hotel. Write 5 sentences asking for things you need. Use numbers with the correct gender and these two prompts:
Eu gostaria de pedir … , por favor.
Por favor, você tem … ?
Example: Eu gostaria de pedir duas toalhas, por favor.
📩 When you're done, send your exercises to your teacher. She will read carefully and give you personalized feedback — pointing out what you did well and helping you improve your Portuguese even further. This exchange is one of the most important parts of the learning process!
