Step 3 — Language Focus

📖 Vocabulário do aeroporto

This episode is packed with essential airport vocabulary you'll need for real travel in Brazil.

Portuguese English Notes
destino destination com destino a = heading to
voo flight voo 783 = flight 783
embarque boarding embarque imediato = immediate boarding
portão gate portão 25 = gate 25
📖 Imperativo negativo — Não se preocupe

B reassures Claudio: "Não se preocupe" — the negative imperative with reflexive verbs like preocupar-se keeps the reflexive pronoun before the verb (unlike the affirmative imperative).

Affirmative Negative English
Preocupe-se! Não se preocupe! Don't worry!
Apresse-se! Não se apresse! Don't rush!
ainda tem tempo there's still time Frase tranquilizadora comum.
✈️ Vamos-lá A casual way to say "let's go" — often used to encourage or hurry someone along, similar to English "come on, let's go!"
