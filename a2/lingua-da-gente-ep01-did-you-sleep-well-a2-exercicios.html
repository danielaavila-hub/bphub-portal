Exercício 1 de 5
Listening Comprehension

Choose the correct answer

Based on the dialogue from Episode 01. If you need to, go back and listen again before answering.

1. How does person A greet person B?
2. What time is it in the dialogue?
3. How does person B describe how they slept?
4. What does "Tá" mean in this context?
Exercício 2 de 5
Gap Fill

Complete the dialogue

Fill in the missing words. Type carefully — accents matter!

A: dia, você bem?
B: Mais ou . Que são?
A: e meia, ainda é muito .
B: Vamos dormir um pouco !
A: , mais um pouquinho só.

Exercício 4 de 5
Grammar Practice

Make suggestions with Vamos

Write a suggestion in Portuguese using Vamos + infinitive. Example: dormir → Vamos dormir um pouco mais!

1. comer
2. trabalhar
3. conversar
4. começar
Exercício 5 de 5
Free Production

Write your own morning dialogue

Write a short dialogue (4–6 lines) between two people in the morning. Use at least 3 expressions from this episode: a greeting, a question about time, a "Vamos…" suggestion, and/or tá / mais ou menos. Be creative — it doesn't have to be about sleeping!

📩 When you're done, send your exercises to your teacher. She will read carefully and give you personalized feedback — pointing out what you did well and helping you improve your Portuguese even further. This exchange is one of the most important parts of the learning process!
